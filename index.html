About Us

At TS Mjandana Trading Construction and Civil Works, we are more than just builders—we are innovators dedicated to shaping the future of infrastructure. Established in 2004, we are a black-owned construction and civil works company specializing in road construction, road painting, house plan design, town planning, and general civil works. With nearly two decades of experience, we have successfully delivered high-quality projects for both government departments and private sector clients, earning a reputation for excellence, reliability, and innovation. Our team of skilled professionals is committed to precision, efficiency, and sustainability, ensuring that every project meets industry standards and exceeds client expectations. At TS Mjandana Trading, we don’t just build structures—we build trust, strong relationships, and thriving communities. Whether you need expert roadworks, architectural planning, or civil engineering solutions, we are here to turn your vision into reality. Let’s build the future together.

Our Services

At TS Mjandana Trading Construction and Civil Works, we offer a wide range of construction and civil engineering solutions tailored to meet the needs of government departments, private developers, and businesses. With expertise in road construction, architectural planning, and general civil works, we are committed to delivering durable, high-quality infrastructure projects.

Road Construction & Painting

We specialize in roadwork construction, road maintenance, and road painting to enhance safety and efficiency. Our road painting services include clear, durable pavement markings that comply with regulatory standards.

House Plan Design & Town Planning

Our expert architects and planners provide custom house designs and town planning services to bring your vision to life. We ensure that every design meets safety standards and municipal regulations.

General Civil Works

From small-scale projects to large infrastructure developments, we provide a variety of civil engineering solutions that support urban and rural development.

ADH Machine Tools & Laser Cutting Solutions

We import and supply high-quality laser cutting machines, as well as cutting sheets and tubes for precision fabrication.
